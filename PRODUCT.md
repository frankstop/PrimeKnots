# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Math learners, curious programmers, and visual thinkers using a desktop browser to understand knot theory through exploration.

## Product Purpose

Prime Knot Periodic Table turns prime knots into a browsable scientific instrument. Success means users can see a scoped knot universe, pick a knot, inspect its identity, and understand which rules accepted it into the table.

## Brand Personality

Precise, curious, calm.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing heroes, fake AI dashboards, neon math wallpaper, unreadable scientific posters, and decoration that competes with knot structure.

## Design Principles

- Scope every claim: infinite theory, finite table.
- Put visual exploration before formulas.
- Show rule evidence near every classification.
- Keep controls dense, stable, and predictable.
- Prefer source-backed labels over ornamental certainty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-friendly controls, readable labels, reduced-motion tolerance, and no color-only meaning.
